Did you know the evolution of car ?

  • Steam-Powered Vehicles

    Early cars were powered by steam engines, such as Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot's 1769 steam-powered tricycle, laying the groundwork for modern automotive engineering.

    18th-19th Century

  • Internal Combustion Engine Cars

    Karl Benz's creation of the Benz Patent-Motorwagen in 1885 introduced gasoline-powered vehicles, leading to mass production.

    Late 19th Century

  • Mass Production and Accessibility

    Henry Ford revolutionized the industry with assembly line production, making cars like the Model T affordable for the average person

    Early 20th Century

  • Technological Advancements

    Cars saw the integration of safety features (e.g., seat belts, airbags), convenience (e.g., automatic transmission, air conditioning), and performance improvements.

    Mid to Late 20th Century

  • Electric and Autonomous Vehicles

    Modern cars emphasize sustainability with electric vehicles (e.g., Tesla) and innovative technologies like self-driving capabilities and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).
